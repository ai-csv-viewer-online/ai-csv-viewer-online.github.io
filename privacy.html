Welcome to AI CSV Viewer Online ("we", "our", or "us"), accessible at https://ai-csv-viewer-online.github.io. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

AI CSV Viewer Online is a 100% client-side tool. This means:

  • Your CSV, TSV, or any other files are never uploaded to any server
  • We do not store any file contents, filenames, or file metadata
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not require account registration or login
  • All data processing (parsing, filtering, sorting, exporting) happens locally in your browser
